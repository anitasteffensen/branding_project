the a team

At A Studio creativity knows no bounds. Our dynamic team comprises four talented and passionate women, who each brings a unique set of skills and perspectives, contributing to the vibrant tapestry that defines our studio.

United by a shared vision for excellence, innovation, and a commitment to empowering creative voices, we thrive in an environment that celebrates diversity and collaboration. We combine our expertise in multimedia production, design, strategy, communication and project management. Together, we form a powerhouse that not only embraces strength and creativity, but also fosters an inclusive and inspiring work culture. Our collective dedication to detail, aesthetic sensibility, and our modern approach sets us apart.

the studio

Nestled in the heart of Copenhagen, our studio serves as a dynamic space where amazing ideas are born. The beautiful surroundings of our studio plays a pivotal role in shaping the creative atmosphere that defines our work. Working in an environment that mirrors the beauty and richness of our visual projects enhances our creative process. As visual creators, we recognize the importance of being immersed in an environment that aligns with the aesthetics we aim to convey.

girl code

In an arena where gender imbalances persist, we embrace the power of diversity and inclusivity as our guiding principles. Our team of women, brings a unique perspective to the forefront, challenging stereotypes and reshaping the narrative. We believe in fostering an environment where creativity knows no gender, and where every voice is not only heard but celebrated. It's more than breaking barriers; it's about rewriting the rules and setting new standards for collaboration, innovation, and success.
